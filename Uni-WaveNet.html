Samples from "Uni-WaveNet: Training IAF WaveNet without knowledge distillation"

Authors:

Ruiqing Xue, Qingsong Liu, Yanhua Long, Jiaen Liang

Abstract:

We propose an efficient training strategy for deep IAF WaveNet, and a carefully designed model with 8-layer IAFs. Each layer consists of a WaveNet with a larger receptive feld. We call this network Uni-WaveNet for short. Compared to Parallel Wavenet and ClariNet, Uni-WaveNet has the following improvements: 1) We can train the IAF WaveNet efciently without knowledge distillation. 2) The structural of the proposed model can meet different computational requirements very well for different applications.
